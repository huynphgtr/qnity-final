Introducing Bãi Xếp – A Hidden Gem in Quy Nhơn

Located about 13 kilometers from downtown Quy Nhơn, Bãi Xếp is one of the region’s most well-known scenic spots. This coastal gem boasts a vibrant seascape full of life, paired with a charming fishing village where you can witness the daily rhythm of local life.

There’s plenty to experience here—swimming in the clear blue waters, snapping Instagram-worthy photos, or learning about the lives of the local fishermen. All of these make Bãi Xếp an unforgettable stop on your Quy Nhơn adventure.

How to Get to Bãi Xếp, Quy Nhơn

Motorbikes, buses, and taxis are the three most popular means of transportation to reach Bãi Xếp. Among them, the bus is a great choice for budget-conscious travelers.

If you choose to travel by bus, catch Route T2 from the Quy Nhơn University gate bus stop. Get off at Khu Phố 2 station, then walk a short distance to reach Bãi Xếp.

For those who prefer more flexibility and want to stop along the way to take photos of the scenic coastal route, traveling by motorbike is the ideal option. You can follow MIA.vn’s suggested route: From Quy Nhơn city center, take National Highway 1D heading toward Phú Yên. After riding for about 17 kilometers, look to your left—you’ll spot the sign leading to Bãi Xếp, Quy Nhơn.

Unforgettable Experiences

3.1 Dive into the Crystal-Clear Waters of Bãi Xếp

With its clear turquoise waters and gentle waves, Bãi Xếp is one of the top spots along the Central Coast for swimming and snorkeling. The underwater world here is truly captivating, home to vibrant coral reefs in all shapes and sizes, as well as a variety of marine life like fish and shrimp.

At Bãi Xếp, you can swim to your heart’s content, letting the cool water and playful waves wash over you. Beyond the sea, the powdery white sand and shady coastal trees offer the perfect setting to relax, sunbathe, or simply enjoy the view. Sit back with a refreshing coconut in hand and take in the stunning natural landscape that stretches out before you.

3.2 Experience the Rhythm of Life in the Fishing Village

To truly understand the daily life of the local fishing community, don’t hesitate to strike up a conversation and join in their activities—pulling in fishing nets, catching fish, or collecting seaweed. These hands-on experiences offer a fresh perspective and promise to make your trip even more memorable.

If you're adventurous, not prone to seasickness, and up for a challenge, hop into a basket boat with the locals and sail out near the shore for a fishing trip. There’s something special about casting a line in the open sea, patiently waiting for a bite, then bringing your fresh catch back to cook up a delicious meal to enjoy with friends.

3.3 Witness the Breathtaking Beauty of Sunrise and Sunset

With no towering buildings to block your view, sunrises and sunsets at Bãi Xếp are truly spectacular—treating you to a dazzling natural light show unlike any other. Head down to the beach and wait for that magical moment.

At sunrise, the first golden rays peek over the horizon, and the fiery red sun slowly rises, breathing life into a quiet new day by the sea. In contrast, as sunset falls, the final beams of sunlight stretch across the sky, casting a warm glow over the landscape. The ocean transforms into a giant mirror reflecting the light, while gentle waves kiss the shoreline—ushering in a peaceful, almost poetic end to the day.

3.4 Savor Delicious Local Cuisine

A visit to Bãi Xếp isn’t complete without indulging in a feast of fresh seafood alongside its stunning scenery and relaxing beach vibes. Seafood here is as fresh as it gets—shrimp, crab, clams, snails, and fish—prepared in a simple yet flavorful way that highlights the natural taste of the ocean.

Beyond seafood, don’t miss the chance to try bánh canh hẹ (a savory chive noodle soup) and chả dông (crispy grilled lizard patties)—local delicacies that are surprisingly tasty!

Tips for Visiting Bãi Xếp, Quy Nhơn

Help protect the beach environment – avoid littering and do not start fires on your own.

Bring sun protection essentials such as sunscreen, umbrellas, hats, and sunglasses.

Swim only in designated areas. Avoid venturing too far from the shore or swimming near warning flags.

Ask for prices in advance to avoid unexpected charges or tourist scams.

If you plan to stay overnight, it's best to book your room early to secure a good rate.
