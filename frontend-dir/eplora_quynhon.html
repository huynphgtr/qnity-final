Introduce to ExploraScience

The Quy Nhơn Science Discovery Center is the first space science center in Vietnam and the second in Southeast Asia (after Singapore). Not only is it an attractive destination with countless Instagram-worthy spots and fascinating spaces, the center sparks a passion for science, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in the wonders of the universe, galaxies, and planets. This refreshing addition makes Quy Nhơn’s travel map more exciting and colorful.

How to Get to the Science Discovery Center

The center is located about 8 kilometers south of Quy Nhơn city center. It’s easy to get there by renting a motorbike or taking a taxi—the journey takes only about 15 minutes. Specifically, from Quy Nhơn Bus Station, Kim Cuc Plaza, or the Ghenh Rang Tien Sa Tourist Area, follow National Highway 1D and look for Science Avenue (Đại lộ Khoa học) on the left-hand side.

A Space Odyssey in the Heart of Quy Nhơn

The Science Discovery Center comprises two main parts: the Main Building and the Public Observatory. The Main Building spans a massive 7,200 m² and houses seven themed exhibition rooms along with a state-of-the-art planetarium dome theater. Each room is designed to ignite curiosity and bring science closer to everyday life.

3.1 Solar System Discovery Room

For space enthusiasts, the Solar System Room feels like stepping into the galaxy. With miniature models of the planets and vibrant representations of the Milky Way, the planetary simulation globe vividly re-creates the surfaces of different celestial bodies, highlighting Earth’s place in the vast universe.

3.2 Matter Discovery Room

Supported by CERN, this room offers an interactive experience where you can have fun while learning. Ever tried playing soccer with protons? The harder you kick, the faster the particles move and collide, simulating real energy creation—who knows, you might even help create the Higgs boson!

3.3 Earth & Natural Resources Room

Focusing on the Earth’s environment and climate change, this room offers an interactive Earth model, insights into environmental challenges, and even experiences like tornado simulations and wind level changes—a moving reminder of our planet’s fragility and the urgent need to protect it.

3.4 Space Exploration Room

If satellites, space missions, and futuristic tech captivate you, this section is a must-see. Divided into themes such as Leaving Earth, Space & Society, and Environmental Monitoring, the exhibits delve deep into humanity’s journey into space and its impact on the world.

3.5 Mars Exploration Room

Dreaming of walking on Mars? This room brings that dream to life with life-sized models of the Mars rover Curiosity, demonstrations of Martian soil analysis, and even comparisons of gravity between Earth and Mars—an essential visit for aspiring astronauts.

3.6 Learn by Playing Room

This interactive room offers fun models that explain real-world scientific principles in surprising ways. With a live stage for science demonstrations, learning here is as entertaining as it is educational.

3.7 Why So? Room

Ever wondered why lightning strikes or how plants grow? This room breaks down everyday natural phenomena into simple, engaging explanations using models and games, showcasing basic concepts in physics, chemistry, and biology.

3.8 Public Observatory

Featuring an 8-meter dome and a 600mm main telescope (CDK600) along with six additional 127mm telescopes, the Public Observatory offers visitors—whether seasoned stargazers or curious beginners—a chance to witness the beauty of nebulae, constellations, and galaxies up close.

Conclusion

We hope the detailed information above helps you plan a truly enriching visit to the Quy Nhơn Science Discovery Center. With its diverse range of interactive exhibits and immersive experiences, this Science Complex stands out as a top destination—especially for families, children, and anyone passionate about astronomy and space exploration.
