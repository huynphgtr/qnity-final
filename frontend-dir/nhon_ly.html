Introducing Nhon Ly Fishing Village

Located about 30 kilometers from downtown Quy Nhơn, Nhon Ly Fishing Village sits peacefully on the Phương Mai Peninsula, in Nhon Ly Commune, Binh Dinh Province. Once a quiet, little-known coastal village, Nhon Ly has recently emerged as an increasingly popular destination—thanks to the breathtaking natural beauty that Mother Nature has generously bestowed upon this land.

What makes Nhon Ly especially charming are the quaint, timeworn houses that stretch along winding village roads, adding a touch of nostalgia to the seaside landscape. If you're a lover of the sea, into snorkeling, or simply looking to immerse yourself in the daily rhythm of a traditional fishing village, Nhon Ly is the perfect spot waiting to be explored.

How to Get to Nhon Ly Fishing Village

Located not too far from the center of Quy Nhơn, Nhon Ly Fishing Village is easily accessible by motorbike or car. For those who love adventure and prefer flexibility, traveling by motorbike is a great option.

You can follow this suggested route by QNITY: Quy Nhơn city center → Thị Nại Bridge → Phương Mai Peninsula → National Highway 19B → Roundabout → FLC Resort → Nhon Ly Fishing Village.

Once you’ve arrived, you can continue exploring the area by motorbike or car. If you're in the mood for a more leisurely experience and want to breathe in the fresh sea air, biking around the village is a perfect way to soak in the surroundings.

Unforgettable Experiences

3.1 Discover the Peaceful Charm of Nhon Ly Fishing Village

As a standout destination on Quy Nhơn’s travel map, Nhon Ly Fishing Village leaves a lasting impression with its serene coastal atmosphere. Nestled between the mountains and the sea, the village’s unique geography creates a breathtaking landscape that is both majestic and romantic.

One of the most striking features of Nhon Ly is its winding, narrow alleyways, thoughtfully designed to block strong sea winds and provide shade. Strolling through these paths, you’ll pass by rows of old-style homes that preserve traditional architecture—with whitewashed walls, red-tiled roofs, and open courtyards, often used for drying fish or hanging laundry. You'll witness the daily rhythm of life in a simpler time as laughter echoes through shaded corners and gentle waves lap in the distance.

3.2 Explore the Picture-Perfect Mural Road

In recent years, Nhon Ly Fishing Village has been brought to life by vibrant mural paintings that adorn its streets, especially in the Dốc Quán area and Chùa village. These murals, inspired by the seaside way of life, depict scenes of fishermen heading out to sea, row bamboo boats through stormy waves, bustling fish markets, and the rich marine life of the area.

Strolling along the mural road offers the perfect opportunity to enjoy the scenery and snap stunning photos. You might even encounter the talented local artists who bring these walls to life.

3.3 Splash Around in the Refreshing Waters

Blessed with crystal-clear waters and gentle waves, Nhon Ly Fishing Village is an ideal spot for a peaceful seaside escape. The underwater ecosystem is diverse—ideal for those who love to swim or snorkel among vibrant coral reefs.

If you prefer to simply relax, you can lounge on the smooth, pristine white sand beach, enjoy the salty sea air, and listen to the soft sounds of waves and rustling leaves, melting away all your stress.

3.4 Stroll Through the Nhon Ly Local Market

One of the oldest markets in the area, the Nhon Ly local market buzzes with activity as early as 5 AM. Here, you can buy fresh seafood and fruits to prepare a home-cooked meal or enjoy a traditional local breakfast made right before your eyes.

This vibrant market offers a true glimpse into the authentic local life and flavors, making it an experience not to be missed.

Travel Guide to Nhon Ly

To fully explore the beauty of Nhon Ly Fishing Village and enjoy its exciting water activities, the best time to visit is from February to May. During this period, the village experiences a cool climate, clear skies, and sunny weather with little rain—perfect for outdoor adventures.

Respect local customs: Nhon Ly is a traditional fishing village, so be mindful of local customs and the serene environment.

Weather conditions: Check the weather forecast before planning your trip, as it can influence your outdoor activities.

Pack essentials: Bring sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, and comfortable shoes for exploring the village and beaches.
